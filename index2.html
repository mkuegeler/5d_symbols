What is PEL?

The pattern expression language (PEL) is a way to express ideas by using simple, domain-independent patterns.

For what is it good for?

PEL makes it more easier to describe complex relationships and reduces the ambiguity of terms whenever people mean the same but use different words

Who is the audience?

Everybody who wants to share ideas with other people but cannot rely on language because of the complexity of the subject.
